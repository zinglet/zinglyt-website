How Zinglyt helps

A community‑centered design that prioritizes privacy, comfort, and intention.

Crush (mutual reveal)

Express private admiration. If both of you tap Crush, you’re revealed to each other—no public exposure, just a warm start and shared context.

Community Confessions

Share anonymous stories, prompts, and shout‑outs verified by campus or work email. A gentle way to spark conversations without names attached.

Privacy, pace, and care

Private until mutual, limited daily introductions, and thoughtful follow‑ups create a safe rhythm for intentional interactions and real chemistry.

Community Confessions 💬

People from the same verified community (like your campus or workplace) can post and read confessions anonymously. Access requires your official community email ID, ensuring that only members of the same circle can participate. It’s a safe, expressive corner to share thoughts, crushes, and moments without revealing your identity — just a little honesty among peers.

Verified communities only • Anonymous by default • Respectful by design

How it works: Verify with your campus/work email • Share anonymously • Read within your circle.

App Description for Search Engines

Zinglyt is a community‑first connection platform for campuses, workplaces, and local circles. Discover intentional interactions through shared belonging—private admiration with Crush (mutual reveal), Community Confessions via verified emails, and curated Zinglyt Events to meet naturally offline. Private until mutual, respectful by design, and built for emotional comfort and safety. Join Zinglyt to create connections, share moments, and find real chemistry in spaces you trust. Available on web and Android across cities like Ahmedabad, Bangalore, and Gurgaon.
